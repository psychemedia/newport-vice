# Preface

This set of storynotes, and the associated repository, represents work-in-progress notes relating to stories from Newport, Isle of Wight, in the 1840s and 1850s.

Elements of the online book pages are rather more polished than the raw original notes in the base repository, although some of the online book pages may also include raw notes.

--Tony "Monty" Hirst

Isle of Wight, 2025
